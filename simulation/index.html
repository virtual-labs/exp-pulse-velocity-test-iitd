ULTRASONIC PULSE TESTING OF CONCRETE


Objective:

To Determine the Quality and the Dynamic Modulus of Elasticity of the Concrete Specimen using Ultrasonic Pulse Velocity method as per IS-516.


Apparatus used:

Transmitting Transducer, Receiving Transducer, Electronic Timing Device, Standard Calibration Bar, Carborundum Stone, Grease


Electronic Timing Device Calibration Bar Carborundum Stone Grease Transmitting
Transducer Receiving Transducer

Step 1: e). Click on Stop Button on Electronic Timing Device to Power Off it.
